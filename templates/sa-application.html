CV & Cover Letter Enhancement Tool

Overview

A straightforward yet powerful tool for enhancing resumes and cover letters, it simplifies the process of enhancing application materials tailored to users needs, whether they are starting their career journey or aiming for the next big opportunity.

Key Features

  • AI-driven customization: Powered by GPT-4 Turbo: It is the engine behind the platform's ability to generate, analyze, and interact with content in a meaningful way.

Technologies Used

  • Programming Language: Python, HTML/CSS, JavaScript
  • Framework: Django
  • Libraries: OpenAI (GPT-4 Turbo)
  • Database: SQLite
  • Integration: Email notifications for password reset

Challenges & Solutions

One of the primary challenges was crafting effective prompts for the GPT engine and ensuring the corpus for CVs and cover letters was accurate, well-structured, and aligned with the correct format for each.
